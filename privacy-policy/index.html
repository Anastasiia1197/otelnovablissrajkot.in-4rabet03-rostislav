4Rabet India Privacy Policy - Your Data Under Reliable Protection

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

At 4Rabet India, we prioritize the protection of your personal data. This privacy policy is designed to clearly explain how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use our website, available to users from India and the IN region. We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws.

What Data We Collect and How It Enhances Your Betting & Gaming Experience

To provide you with the best services, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Data Provided Voluntarily: When creating an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and other information necessary to provide services to users in India.
  • Usage and Visit Data: We collect information about your interaction with our site, including IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and session duration.
  • Transaction Data: Information about deposits and withdrawals in Indian Rupees (₹), processed through secure payment gateways. We do not store your full bank card details.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and app version (if using the 4Rabet app).

This data allows us to:

  1. Personalize your betting and gaming experience and offer relevant bonuses

  2. Continuously improve the functionality and security of the 4Rabet India platform

  3. Provide effective and fast support to users from India

  4. Guarantee the security of your account and prevent fraud

  5. Inform you about new games, cricket betting markets, and promotions (only with your consent)

The Role of Cookies in the Operation and Personalization of 4Rabet India

Cookies are small text files saved on your device to improve your website experience. We use the following types:

Cookie Type Purpose of Use Storage Duration
Essential Cookies Basic website functionality (login, session security) Until the end of session or 12 months
Performance / Analytical Cookies Traffic analysis and website improvement 24 months
Functionality Cookies Remembering your preferences (language, currency ₹, bet slip) 12 months
Advertising / Targeting Cookies Showing relevant advertising (only with consent) 12 months

You can manage cookie settings through your browser. Disabling some of them may limit the functionality of the 4Rabet India website.

How We Responsibly Use Your Data to Develop Our Service

We process your data strictly in accordance with the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contract Performance: To provide gaming and betting services, process transactions in ₹, and manage your account.
  • Legitimate Interest: To analyze and improve the platform, ensure its security, and protect against fraudulent activities.
  • Consent: To send promotional and informational materials, if you have given explicit consent.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal requirements, including anti-money laundering rules applicable in India.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to achieve the stated purposes, or for the period required by the laws of India.

Strict Data Transfer Protocols to Trusted Partners

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share information with:

  1. Technical Partners: Companies that help operate the site (hosting, analytics, payment systems like those supporting UPI) subject to their confidentiality commitments.

  2. Legal Authorities: In cases provided by the laws of India, or upon request from authorized bodies.

  3. Affiliated Parties: Only with your explicit prior consent.

All our partners are carefully vetted and contractually obligated to protect your data. When transferring data outside India, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

Your Rights to Access, Correct, or Delete Data

In accordance with the law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Obtain confirmation of the processing of your data and access to it.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing in specific situations.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for compelling reasons.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@4rabet-india.example.com. We will respond within 30 calendar days in accordance with legal requirements for users in India.

Notification of Privacy Policy Updates

We are committed to informing you of any material changes to this policy. Updates will be published on this page with the date of the last revision. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through a prominent notice on the website.

We recommend periodically reviewing this policy to stay informed about how we protect your information. Continued use of the 4Rabet India services after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the new version.
